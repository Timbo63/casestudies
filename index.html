Expanding Pediatric Device Sales Across EMEA

Strategic market entry and distribution network development for MD Orthopaedics

Business Challenge
MD Orthopaedics, a global leader in Ponseti Method clubfoot orthoses and splints, needed to establish and expand their presence across diverse EMEA healthcare markets, each with unique regulatory frameworks and reimbursement models.
Solution Implementation
As EMEA Sales Representative & Business Analyst, managed sales operations and distributor networks, conducted TAM/SAM/SOM assessments, and aligned pricing with regional regulatory requirements and reimbursement frameworks.
Business Outcomes & Impact
33% year-over-year sales growth in key international markets
Established scalable distributor partnerships for long-term market stability
Strengthened regional presence across multiple EMEA markets
Improved market access for pediatric mobility solutions

Forecasting & Sales Planning Enablement

Cross-functional alignment for operational excellence and revenue growth

Business Challenge
Medical device businesses needed to improve forecast reliability and unify planning across sales, supply chain management, and finance teams to enhance revenue predictability and optimize resource allocation.
Solution Implementation
Developed data-driven sales forecasting models, created business intelligence dashboards to monitor sales KPIs, and implemented integrated team alignment processes to streamline operations across previously siloed departments.
Business Outcomes & Impact
$1.4M+ revenue growth by optimizing sales and distribution strategies
Improved decision-making through enhanced data visibility
Reduced operational challenges with better planning
Established shared operational rhythm across teams
